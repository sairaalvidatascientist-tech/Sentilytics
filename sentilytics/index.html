About the Department of Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science at the University of Layyah stands as a beacon of technological innovation and academic excellence in the region. Established with a vision to nurture the next generation of tech leaders, our department combines cutting-edge curriculum with hands-on research opportunities.

Our faculty comprises distinguished researchers and industry experts who are committed to fostering critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and innovation. We offer comprehensive programs in artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, cybersecurity, and software engineering, preparing students for the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Through state-of-the-art laboratories, industry partnerships, and collaborative research projects like Sentilytics, we empower students to transform theoretical knowledge into practical solutions that address real-world challenges. Our graduates have gone on to make significant contributions in leading tech companies, research institutions, and entrepreneurial ventures worldwide.
