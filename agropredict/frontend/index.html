AgroPredict Institutional Statement

AgroPredict integrates plant phenotyping data, environmental monitoring variables, and machine learning algorithms to enable early-stage prediction of crop growth and yield outcomes. The platform is designed to support agricultural researchers, plant breeders, and data scientists at the University of Layyah in making transparent, reproducible, and evidence-based decisions across crop production systems.
